# Shinhan Bank Leave Policy

## Annual Leave

All full-time employees are entitled to annual leave based on their years of service:
- 1-3 years: 15 days per year
- 3-5 years: 18 days per year
- 5-10 years: 21 days per year
- 10+ years: 25 days per year

Annual leave must be requested at least 5 business days in advance through the HR portal. 
Managers must approve within 2 business days.

## Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per year.
- For absences of 3+ consecutive days, a medical certificate is required.
- Unused sick leave does not carry over to the next year.

## Parental Leave

### Maternity Leave
- 90 days of paid maternity leave
- Can begin up to 30 days before the expected delivery date
- Additional 30 days of unpaid leave available upon request

### Paternity Leave
- 10 days of paid paternity leave
- Must be taken within 90 days of the child's birth

## Special Leave

- Marriage: 5 days
- Bereavement (immediate family): 5 days
- Bereavement (extended family): 3 days
- Moving: 1 day (once per year)

## Public Holidays

Shinhan Bank observes all national public holidays as designated by the Korean government.
Employees required to work on public holidays will receive 1.5x overtime pay.

## Leave Approval Process

1. Submit request via Shinhan HR Portal
2. Direct manager reviews and approves/rejects within 2 business days
3. HR department records the approved leave
4. Employee receives confirmation email
5. Leave balance is automatically updated
