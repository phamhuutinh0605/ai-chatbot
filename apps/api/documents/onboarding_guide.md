# Shinhan Bank New Employee Onboarding Guide

## Week 1: Orientation

### Day 1
- Welcome session with HR (9:00 AM - 10:00 AM)
- ID card and access badge issuance
- IT equipment setup (laptop, phone, monitors)
- Office tour and facility introduction
- Meet your team and direct manager

### Day 2-3
- Shinhan Bank history and culture presentation
- Compliance and ethics training (mandatory)
- IT security awareness training (mandatory)
- Anti-money laundering (AML) training

### Day 4-5
- Department-specific training begins
- System access and tool onboarding
- Introduction to team projects and workflows

## Week 2-4: Training Period

- Shadowing senior team members
- Completing online training modules on Shinhan Academy
- Setting up quarterly goals with manager
- First 1-on-1 meeting with department head

## Required Documents

Please prepare the following for HR on Day 1:
1. Government-issued ID (original + copy)
2. Bank account details (Shinhan Bank account preferred)
3. Educational certificates
4. Previous employment certificates
5. Emergency contact information
6. Signed employment contract

- Standard probation: 3 months
- Performance review at 1 month and 3 months
- Successful completion leads to permanent employment status
