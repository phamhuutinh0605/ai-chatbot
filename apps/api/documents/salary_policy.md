# Shinhan Bank Salary & Compensation Policy

## Salary Structure

### Base Salary Grades
Shinhan Bank uses a grade-based salary system:

| Grade | Position Level | Monthly Range (KRW) |
|-------|---------------|---------------------|
| G1    | Junior Staff  | 3,000,000 - 3,800,000 |
| G2    | Staff         | 3,800,000 - 4,800,000 |
| G3    | Senior Staff  | 4,800,000 - 6,200,000 |
| G4    | Assistant Manager | 6,200,000 - 7,800,000 |
| G5    | Manager       | 7,800,000 - 10,000,000 |
| G6    | Senior Manager| 10,000,000 - 13,000,000 |
| G7    | Director      | 13,000,000+ |

## Performance Bonus

Annual performance bonuses are paid in February based on the previous year's evaluation:
- S (Exceptional): 300% of monthly salary
- A (Excellent): 200% of monthly salary
- B (Good): 150% of monthly salary
- C (Satisfactory): 100% of monthly salary
- D (Needs Improvement): 50% of monthly salary

## Salary Review

- Annual salary reviews are conducted in January
- Adjustments are effective from March 1st
- Average annual increase: 3-5% based on performance and market conditions

## Overtime Policy

- Overtime is calculated at 1.5x the regular hourly rate
- Weekend work is calculated at 2.0x the regular hourly rate
- Public holiday work is calculated at 2.5x the regular hourly rate
- Maximum overtime: 52 hours per month

## Benefits & Allowances

### Transportation
- Monthly transportation allowance: 100,000 KRW

### Meals
- Lunch subsidy: 10,000 KRW per working day
- Company cafeteria available at headquarters

### Health Insurance
- Company covers 50% of National Health Insurance premium
- Additional private health insurance provided for Grade G4 and above

### Retirement
- National Pension: Company matches employee contribution
- Corporate pension plan: Additional 5% of monthly salary contributed by company

## Payment Schedule

- Salary is paid on the 25th of each month
- If the 25th falls on a weekend/holiday, payment is made on the preceding business day
- Bonuses are paid on the 15th of the applicable month
